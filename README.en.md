## 🌿 What it is

> *A living gamification that channels habit changes, connects people and generates permanent coordination flows for cultural and environmental regeneration.*

The **Jogo da Agroecologia** (Agroecology Game) is a Pedagogical Tool and Social Technology developed to accelerate the Agroecological Transition. It was built from real transition practices, with the direct participation of the people involved, and is replicable across different territorial contexts.

It can be played by civil society individuals and by institutions — private, governmental, or commons-based.

Gamification is used as a pedagogical strategy for engagement, experiential learning and critical reflection — not as a competitive mechanism or behavioral control tool.

## 🌍 What we understand by Agroecology

> Integration of interdisciplinary sciences, sustainable productive practices and social movement — articulating scientific knowledge and traditional wisdom to build fair, resilient and ecologically balanced food systems.

The **Agroecological Transition** is not limited to technical changes in productive systems. It involves cultural, social, economic and institutional transformations, requiring collective and structural processes.

## 🌱 Philosophy

We understand that lasting cultural changes depend on the transformation of social and institutional structures. Isolated individual actions are insufficient to face systemic challenges.

The Jogo da Agroecologia does not aim to replace public policies, social movements or existing productive practices — we propose to act as a **pedagogical and cultural device** that integrates and strengthens initiatives already underway in Brazil and other territories.

> *"For other forms of cultures to continue existing or to emerge, we need permanent coordination flows that sustain life."*
