# 🌱 Jogo da Agroecologia

{% hint style="info" %}
**Pedagogical Tool · Social Technology · Regenerative Platform**
From agroecological transition to permanent coordination — playing to regenerate life.
{% endhint %}

The **Jogo da Agroecologia** (Agroecology Game) is a Pedagogical Tool developed to accelerate the Agroecological Transition.

It is also a **Social Technology**: built from real agroecological transition practices, with the direct participation of the people involved, presenting a replicable character in different territorial contexts and promoting social transformation through collective educational processes.

***

## 👩‍🌾 About the creator

{% hint style="success" %}
Developed by **Tainá Moraes Buzzatti** — Brazilian, mother, teacher, agroecological activist, feminist and communist/anarchist. Born in June 1994.

The Jogo da Agroecologia emerges from a situated knowledge, built from direct experience with education, agroecology and socio-environmental activism — conferring practical legitimacy and coherence between personal trajectory, pedagogical proposal and political positioning.
{% endhint %}
